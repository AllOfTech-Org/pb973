About Us

About Us

PB(973) is a pickleball club built for players who value the game and competition. Our flagship experience blends championship courts with seamless booking, curated leagues, and a Players' Lounge designed for connection.

Tony Corbo, in partnership with NextPlay Sports Development LLC, is bringing an entirely new pickleball experience to North Jersey — one unlike anything Jerseyans have ever seen. Backed by NextPlay Sports proven expertise in real estate development and sports facilities, the team is reimagining what a modern athletic and social destination can be.

Their shared vision goes beyond courts and competition, focusing on creating an energetic, welcoming environment where people don't just play they stay, connect, and become part of something bigger. Every space is designed to feel active, social, and community-driven, transforming pickleball facilities into true gathering places.

As managing partner and pickleball expert, Tony brings extensive experience in programming and operations. His leadership ensures excellence at every level — from court quality and league organization to clinics, junior development, and the overall player experience. That same commitment defines the partnership and sets the standard for the PB(973) brand.

That vision comes to life with PB(973) – Play the Code, a premium indoor pickleball social club opening soon in Flanders. Built around pickleball, PB(973) introduces a vibrant social club atmosphere featuring Ping Pong, Mah Jong, canasta, billiards, and inviting gathering spaces that turn every visit into more than just a match.

Frequently Asked
Questions

Everything you need to know about memberships, bookings, courts, and the PB(973) experience — answered clearly.

No — you do not need a paid membership to play. With the Free Code Access Membership, you can pay as you go. Or upgrade to our annual Code Priority or Code Senior 65+ tiers for advanced booking windows, discounted court fees, and more.

All memberships are valid for 1 year from the day PB(973) opens, or from the day payment is made after opening.

Yes, cancellations are allowed under the following conditions:

  • No charge if management cancels due to weather or unforeseen issues.
  • No charge with 12+ hours' notice for open play.
  • No charge with 24+ hours' notice for clinics and private lessons.
  • Private Court Rentals cannot be cancelled but may be rescheduled with 48 hours' notice.

Yes — you can select your preferred court in the app before finalizing payment, provided it's available. If not, the system will automatically assign you a court.

No. You may not use any court without a valid reservation for that specific court. Jumping to unreserved courts will result in a fine. You can, however, check if the court is available and book it on the spot.

No — coaches not on the official PB(973) coaching roster are not permitted to charge for lessons on our courts. If you're interested in coaching here, please contact us to discuss a partnership.

You may bring your own, but no food or colored drinks are allowed on the playing courts. Water is permitted courtside. Vending machines and a hydration station are available throughout the facility.

We use a player-share model — the court cost is split among who's playing:

4 Code Access Members $52 total ($13/person)
3 Code Access + 1 Code Priority $13/hr + $10/hr respectively
Code Priority Senior 65+ $46/hr court rental

The app automatically calculates each player's share based on their membership tier.
